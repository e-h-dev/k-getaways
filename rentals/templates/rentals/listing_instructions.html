How to Create Your Listing

  • Sign In:
    All owners must sign in to list a home. Clicking the "List Home" button below will redirect you to log in or register for an account.
  • Complete the form:
    Fill in all required fields accurately.
  • Select amenities:
    Showcase what your property offers by selecting amenities from the list.
  • Set rental price:
    Set your rental price using one of five flexible options: daily, Shabbos, weekly, monthly, or a flat rate for your entire available duration.
  • Manage availability:
    Your property defaults to available; you can add unavailable dates later.
  • Label your photos:
    Upload as many images as you like, but ensure each has a name or explanation.
  • Review listing fees:
    Listing your house costs a flat fee equal to 50% of one night's rate (free if listed before שבעה עשר בתמוז).
  • Track expiration:
    Your property listing will remain valid and live for 30 days.
  • Extend your listing:
    If you would like to list for longer contact us at office@koshergetaways.co.uk.
  • Edit your listing:
    You can edit all sections of your listing except for the price. To update your listing, log into your account and hover over the account navigation bar. Next, select Your Rentals from the drop-down menu. Once your rental loads, click the Edit This Rental link and complete the form.
